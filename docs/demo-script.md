# 2-Minute Demo Recording Script — OpNarrate AI

**Tooling suggestion:** Loom, OBS, or QuickTime screen recording. Record your screen (browser window) plus your voice narration. Target run-time: 1:45–2:00.

| Time | Screen action | Narration |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:15 | Show the live URL loading in an incognito window | "This is OpNarrate AI, a tool I built to help OB-GYN surgeons generate evidence-based operative technique and findings notes right after a case, instead of spending fifteen to twenty minutes writing them from memory." |
| 0:15–0:35 | Select "Cesarean Section" from the procedure dropdown; fill patient data fields quickly | "I select the procedure — here, a Cesarean section — and enter the patient's data, diagnoses, and surgical team once. This same data will populate both the technique and findings sections." |
| 0:35–0:55 | Click "Start Dictation," speak a short case summary, click "Stop Dictation" | "Instead of typing everything, I can dictate what I actually did in the OR — for example: 'Performed a primary low transverse cesarean section for arrest of dilatation, delivered a live baby boy, placenta complete, estimated blood loss five hundred milliliters, no complications.'" |
| 0:55–1:10 | Click "Generate Operative Technique & Findings"; show loading state | "When I submit, the AI drafts both sections using a curated, evidence-based step template for this specific procedure, combined with what I just dictated." |
| 1:10–1:40 | Scroll through the generated Operative Technique and Operative Findings | "Here's the generated draft — a full blow-by-blow operative technique, and a separate operative findings summary. Note the banner: this is clearly labeled as an AI-generated draft requiring physician review — I can edit it directly before copying or printing it." |
| 1:40–1:55 | Show the Copy button and Print button | "I can copy either section to paste into the hospital record system, or print/export directly." |
| 1:55–2:00 | Close on the homepage | "That's OpNarrate AI — a focused, template-grounded documentation accelerator for OB-GYN, not a generic chatbot." |

## After Recording
1. Upload to Loom / YouTube (unlisted) / Google Drive with link sharing enabled.
2. Test the link in an incognito/private window before submission to confirm it plays without a login prompt.
3. Paste the final link into `README.md` under "Demo video" and into your assignment submission.
